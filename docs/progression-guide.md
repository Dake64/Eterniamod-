# Eternia - Progression Guide

A player-facing walkthrough of the intended path through Eternia. (Balance and art are still in
development; numbers here describe intent, not final tuning.)

## 1. Get a Soul

Everything starts with the **Eternal**, Eternia's town NPC. Talk to him and take an **Empty Soul**.
Craft it (in your inventory) into one base class:

- **Warrior** - melee
- **Mage** - magic
- **Ranger** - ranged
- **Summoner** - summons

Equip the class Soul in an accessory slot to activate the class.

### The Soul rules (important)

- A body always needs a Soul. Owning a class Soul but **not keeping one equipped** inflicts a
  severe penalty (huge damage/defense/speed loss).
- While a class Soul is active, **using a weapon of a different class kills you.** Stay on-class.

## 2. Level up and shape your build

- Killing enemies grants **EXP** while a class Soul is active. Each level gives **stat points** and
  **passive points**.
- Open the panels: **Stats (L)**, **Passives (J)**, **Soul (K)**.
- Spend passive points down a **branch**. Every passive feeds an **affinity**, and your dominant
  affinity is what decides your subclass later. The Eternal's **"Read my soul"** tells you where you
  are heading before it is locked in.

## 3. Gear up (pre-Hardmode)

- Each class has a full pre-Hardmode arsenal. The Eternal stocks starter gear for your Soul.
- Brew **class potions** at a bottle: the base-class brews (melee/magic/ranged/summon), the Battle
  Tonic, the Eternal Feast, and the Warding Tonic (defense, great before a boss).
- **Subclass mechanic tonics** (Coolant, Adrenaline, Focus, Warcry, Bloodlust, Overdrive) buff a
  specific subclass mechanic once you have chosen a direction.

## 4. Prototype-01 (Eternia's pre-Hardmode boss)

- Buy the **Corrupted Soul Core** from the Eternal (or craft it from salvaged tech), then use it to
  summon **Prototype-01**.
- It is a fast, three-phase weapon-module machine. Watch its **Soul core**: it grows brighter and
  the machine breaks apart as its health drops, and phase 3 is the most aggressive.
- Drops: **Prototype Core** (advanced gear material), **Soul Alloy** (Soul Ascension), and the
  **Soulforged Sabre**.

## 5. Ascend your Soul

- Craft **Soul Ascension** at a Demon Altar from **Soul Alloy** + **Prototype Core**.
- Each use permanently raises your **Soul tier** (up to 5), adding class damage, crit, max life and
  defense. It survives a Soul Reforge, so it is pure permanent progress. Farm Prototype-01 for the
  Soul Alloy.

## 6. Wall of Flesh - the Awakening

- Defeat the **Wall of Flesh**. Whichever affinity you fed the most seals your **promotion** into one
  of twelve subclasses, and the **Awakening ceremony** plays -- naming your subclass and the new
  mechanic you now carry.
- Locked in the wrong subclass? The Eternal sells the **Soul Reforge** in Hardmode: it wipes your
  passives and affinities so your subclass is decided again. It is deliberately expensive.

## 7. Hardmode and Prototype-02

- Your subclass arsenal continues into Hardmode, shaped by the subclass's signature mechanic.
- Craft the **Awakened Soul Core** (or buy it from the Eternal in Hardmode) to summon **Prototype-02**
  -- sturdier, faster, and its core already ruptures in phase 2. Drops the **Refined Prototype Core**
  and the **Soulforged Greatsaber**.

## 8. The Boss Codex (N)

- Press **N** for the **Boss Codex**: every boss on the road, filtered by tier or defeated status.
- For bosses you have beaten it records your **kill count**, **fastest clear**, and the **highest
  rarity** you ever faced them at, plus each boss's **real drops and drop rates**.
