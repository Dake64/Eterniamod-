Eternia is an RPG overhaul built around Soul-bound class identity. Begin with an Empty Soul and bind it to Warrior, Mage, Ranger or Summoner, then grow through combat EXP, stats and passive affinities. The branch you feed decides your Hardmode promotion into one of twelve subclasses, each with its own mechanic, arsenal and way of earning its power. Face Eternia's own boss Prototype-01, track every kill in the Boss Codex, brew class potions, and ascend your Soul itself.
