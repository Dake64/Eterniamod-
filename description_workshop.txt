Eternia is an early RPG overhaul for tModLoader focused on Soul-bound class identity.

Core loop:
- Begin with an Empty Soul, then craft it into Warrior, Mage, Ranger or Summoner.
- Level through combat EXP while a class Soul is active.
- Spend stat points and passive points to shape your build.
- After Wall of Flesh, your dominant passive affinity determines your promotion.

Current promotion direction:
- Warrior paths include Swordsman, Fighter, Guardian, Yoyo Master, Berserker and Stunner.
- Mage paths include Elementalist, Cursed Mage, Infinity Mage and Arcane Bard.
- Ranger paths include Energy Gunner, Archer, Gunner and Virtuoso.
- Summoner paths include Beast Tamer, Advanced Summoner, Tech Summoner and Necromancer.

Soul rules are strict by design: using the wrong weapon class while bound to a class Soul is heavily punished, and owning a class Soul without keeping one active weakens the player.

This is a development build focused on the foundation, UI rework, class progression and a playable vertical slice.
